Privacy Policy

Context Keeper Chrome Extension

Last Updated: January 19, 2026

TL;DR: Context Keeper stores all your data locally on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or sell any of your information. Your privacy is our priority.

1. Introduction

Context Keeper ("we," "our," or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Chrome extension.

2. Information Collection

2.1 Data We Collect

Context Keeper collects and stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Tab URLs and Titles: When you save a workspace, we store the URLs and titles of your open tabs
  • Workspace Names: Custom names you assign to your saved workspaces
  • Workspace Metadata: Creation dates, last modified dates, and tab counts
  • User Preferences: Settings and preferences you configure within the extension

2.2 Data We DO NOT Collect

Context Keeper does not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Payment or financial information
  • Browsing history outside of saved workspaces
  • Passwords or authentication credentials
  • Geolocation data
  • Analytics or usage statistics

3. Data Usage

3.1 How We Use Your Information

All information collected by Context Keeper is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To save and restore your browser tab workspaces
  • To provide workspace management functionality
  • To remember your preferences and settings
  • To display your saved workspaces in the extension interface

3.2 How We DO NOT Use Your Information

We do not:

  • Analyze your data for marketing purposes
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Sell or monetize your data
  • Use your data to train AI models
  • Track your browsing behavior

4. Data Storage

4.1 Storage Location

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. Specifically:

  • Data is stored in chrome.storage.local
  • Storage is managed by the Chrome browser on your device
  • Data never leaves your computer

4.2 Storage Duration

  • Workspace data is stored indefinitely until you manually delete it
  • You can clear all data at any time through the extension or by uninstalling
  • Uninstalling the extension removes all stored data from your browser

5. Data Handling

5.1 Data Transmission

Context Keeper does NOT transmit any data over the internet. The extension operates entirely offline and does not:

  • Send data to remote servers
  • Use third-party analytics services
  • Track your browsing activity
  • Share information with advertisers
  • Collect telemetry or usage statistics
  • Connect to external APIs or databases

5.2 Data Sharing

We do NOT share your data with anyone. This includes:

  • No sharing with third-party services
  • No sharing with analytics providers
  • No sharing with advertising networks
  • No sharing with data brokers
  • No sharing with other extensions or applications

6. Permissions Explanation

6.1 Storage Permission

Why we need it: Required to save workspace data locally on your device.

How we use it: To store and retrieve your saved workspaces. This data never leaves your computer.

6.2 Tabs Permission

Why we need it: Required to access tab URLs and titles when saving workspaces, and to open tabs when restoring workspaces.

How we use it: Only when you explicitly save or restore a workspace. We don't monitor your tabs otherwise.

6.3 Side Panel Permission

Why we need it: Required to display the extension's interface in Chrome's side panel.

How we use it: To provide a better user experience by integrating with Chrome's UI.

7. Data Security

7.1 Security Measures

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data is protected by your device's security measures
  • No data is at risk of server breaches or hacks
  • No network transmission means no man-in-the-middle attacks
  • You have complete control over your data

7.2 Your Responsibility

To protect your data:

  • Keep your browser and extension updated
  • Use a strong device password/PIN
  • Log out of shared or public computers
  • Review your saved workspaces periodically

8. User Rights and Controls

8.1 Your Data Rights

You have complete control over your data and the right to:

  • Access: View all your stored workspaces through the extension
  • Export: Export your workspace data (feature available in extension)
  • Delete: Remove individual workspaces or all data at once
  • Modify: Edit workspace names and settings at any time

8.2 How to Exercise Your Rights

To view your data: Open Context Keeper extension and browse your workspaces

To delete your data:

  • Option 1: Delete individual workspaces within the extension
  • Option 2: Uninstall the extension (removes all stored data)
  • Option 3: Clear Chrome's extension storage data manually

9. Third-Party Services

Context Keeper does not use any third-party services. This includes:

  • No analytics tools (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • No tracking mechanisms
  • No external APIs
  • No advertising networks
  • No cloud storage services

The extension is completely self-contained and operates entirely offline.

10. Children's Privacy

Context Keeper does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since all data is stored locally and never transmitted, there are no age restrictions for using this extension.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child is using this extension, you can remove it from their browser at any time.
